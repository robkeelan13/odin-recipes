Chicken Alfredo

Description

This is a deliciously creamy pasta dish that is quick and easy to make. Your friends and family will be amazed at your kitchen wizardry.

The dish features some simple ingredients with the pasta, chicken, peas, and creamy sauce being the heroes of the meal.

Ingredients

  • 300g Chicken Breast
  • 250ml Cooking Cream
  • 500g Fettucine Pasta
  • 200g Frozen Peas
  • 3 Cloves of Garlic
  • 1 Shallot
  • 150g Parmessan

Steps

  1. Finely chop the garlic and shallot.
  2. Chop the chicken breast into small, bite-sized pieces.
  3. Heat some olive oil in a large frying pan.
  4. Brown the chicken breast, remove from the pan, and set aside.
  5. Add the pasta to a saucepan of boiling water.
  6. Heat more olive oil in the frying pan and add the garlic and shallot for one minute until fragrant.
  7. Add the cooking cream to the frying pan and stir to combine with the garlic and shallot for one minute.
  8. Add the frozen peas and the chicken you set aside to the frying pan and combine with the sauce for one minute.
  9. Drain the water from the pasta,add the pasta to the frying pan and stir to combine.
  10. Add the parmessan to the frying pan and combine for 1 minute.
  11. Serve in a bowl.

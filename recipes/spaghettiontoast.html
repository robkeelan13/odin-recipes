Spaghetti on Toast

Description

It's hard to go wrong with this one. What can I say, sometimes a body just needs some old fashioned spag on toast!

Ingredients

  • 1 tin of spaghetti
  • 2 slices of white bread
  • Butter for toast

Steps

  1. Pop the bread in the toaster.
  2. While the bread is toasting, microwave the spaghetti for 1 minute.
  3. When the bread is toasted, butter it and cut each slice in half. Put it on a plate.
  4. Stir the spaghetti and then microwave for another 30 seconds.
  5. Pour the spaghetti on the toast to serve.
